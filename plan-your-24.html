Refund Policy

    • In the event that you cancel your reservation for any reason, we will allow credit for payment in the form of an account credit, which can be applied to another tour. Credit will be given with a minimum of two weeks notice prior to the day of the tour. Please note that no credit will be given less than two weeks prior to the start of your tour.

Weather Hotline

    • On the day of your tour, call our weather hotline at 312-799-2002 to get the most up to date information, if weather conditions are cause for concern.

Getting to the Museum

    • The Museum is located at 1601 North Clark Street at the South end of Lincoln Park on the corner of Clark and North Avenue.

    • From Interstate 90/94

    • Exit at North Avenue, drive east to LaSalle Street and turn left. Make a left on Stockton Drive to the parking garage.

    • From Lake Shore Drive

    • Exit at North Avenue and drive west. Turn right on Stockton Drive to the parking garage.

    • By CTA

    • The Museum is easy to reach via public transportation. CTA buses 22, 36, 72, 73, 151, and 156 stop nearby. The Brown Line Sedgwick station and Red Line Clark/Division station are also located approximately one half-mile from the Museum. For travel information, call 312-836-7000 or visit transitchicago.com.

Parking

    • Public parking is conveniently located one block north of the Museum at Clark and LaSalle streets. Enter on Stockton Drive.

    • Parking Cost

    • $9 with Museum validation; Visa, Mastercard, and Discover credit cards are accepted.

    • Parking Accessibility

    • Specially located spots have been reserved for persons using mobility devices. Call 312-642-4600 for more information.

Museum Accessibility

    • All public spaces and most restrooms are wheelchair accessible. A limited number of wheelchairs and a listening system are available on gallery tours.
