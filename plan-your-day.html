Tour Information

Refund Policy

    • In the event that you cancel your reservation for any reason, we will allow credit for payment in the form of an account credit, which can be applied to another tour. Credit will be given with a minimum of two weeks notice prior to the day of the tour. Please note that no credit will be given less than two weeks prior to the start of your tour.

Weather Hotline

    • On the day of the tour, call our weather hotline to get the most up to date information about your tour. The weather hotline is 312-799-2002.

Museum Information

Getting to the Museum

    • Museum Address

    • The Museum is located at 1601 North Clark Street at the South end of Lincoln Park on Clark and North Avenue.

    • From Interstate 90/94

    • Exit at North Avenue, drive east to LaSalle Street and turn left. Make a left on Stockton Drive to the parking garage.

    • From Lake Shore Drive

    • Exit at North Avenue and drive west. Turn right on Stockton Drive to the parking garage.

    • By CTA

    • CTA buses 22, 36, 72, 73, 151 and 156 all stop nearby. For travel information, call 312-836-7000 or visit transitchicago.com.

Parking

    • Public parking is conveniently located one block north of the Museum at Clark and LaSalle streets. Enter on Stockton Drive. Parking validation and handicapped parking are available.

Accessibility

    • All public spaces and most restrooms are wheelchair accessible. A limited number of wheelchairs and a listening system are available on gallery tours.
